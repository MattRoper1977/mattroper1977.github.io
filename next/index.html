The promises underneath everything

🔒 Nothing uploaded, ever

No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Registers and evidence live on the school device and nowhere else — data protection by architecture, not by policy.

📄 Paperwork under your header

The UAS and ASDAN registers print claim documents, audits and tutor records under your own school's name, ready for the file.

📶 Offline-first

Every lesson, game and tool is a single file that keeps working when the Wi-Fi doesn't. Built for real classrooms, not demo conditions.

🆓 Free, teacher-built

Made by a working SEMH teacher, shared free. No premium tier, no locked features, no invoice.

"The site never asks who a child is — and that single decision answers most of the questions a governing body would ever ask about it."

For leaders, governors, trusts & local authorities

What you can verify, not just admire

No dashboards and no portals — deliberately. Everything a leadership audience needs is inspectable in the open.

Evidence trails that stand up

The ASDAN and UAS registers keep dated, attributable records — hours, photo evidence, sign-offs — and export them as school-headed documents for claims, EHCP reviews and audits.

Curriculum you can trace

Primary science follows White Rose sequencing; vocational provision maps to ASDAN PEQ and AQA Unit Awards. Every scheme of work is published and readable.

Safeguarding-conscious by design

Projector privacy modes, no pupil accounts, no data leaving the building — the risk assessment mostly writes itself.

Total cost: £0

No procurement, no licences, no renewal dates. A school can adopt everything this afternoon and spend nothing.
