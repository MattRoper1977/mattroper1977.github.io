What it does

Reads the unit for you

Paste the AQA sheet, upload the PDF, or point a camera at a photocopy — it finds the unit code, title, level and every outcome, and lets you check before saving.

Tap-to-stamp register

Pick an outcome, tap a pupil, and today's date is inked against their name. Attendance mode too. The coverage grid shows every gap before you claim.

Work examples with real references

Snap a photo of the work and it files itself with an AQA-style reference, per outcome, automatically.

The official summary sheet, filled in

The genuine AQA landscape summary sheet — centre, unit, learner numbers, dates — completed from your register. It even warns you, by name, if anyone's missing an outcome.

One PDF, ready to email

Cover page, summary sheet and every work example in a single download, named and ready to attach for your co-ordinator.

Yours, privately

Everything stays in your browser on your machine — no accounts, no cloud, no pupil data leaving the room. One-click backup keeps it safe.

Worth knowing: your register lives in this browser on this computer. Export a backup from Settings after big marking sessions — especially before laptops get reimaged over the holidays. The scan reader (OCR) and the PDF download each fetch their engine from the internet the first time they're used; everything else works fully offline.
