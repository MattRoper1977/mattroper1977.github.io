What it does

Two ASDAN shapes, one app

PEQ units track knowledge criteria, logged GLH hours and delivery milestones. Short Courses track modules, Section A/B challenges and hour-based credits.

The W·P·R trio

Every challenge needs Work evidence, a Planning sheet and a Review sheet before ASDAN counts it. Three taps per challenge — and hours only bank when all three are there.

Honest credit maths

Credits are hours ÷ 10, whole credits only — exactly how ASDAN claims them. The app tells you how far the next credit is.

The readiness board

Per pupil, green-amber-red: green means claim today; amber means credits banked but the summary of achievement or personal statement is still missing — named, so nothing slips.

Work examples with references

Snap a photo and it files itself against the challenge or criterion.

FW/M2C3/A

Moderation paperwork, printed

Per pupil: the portfolio audit grid, the hours tally sheet and a signed tutor record — front-of-folder ready.
